WHO WE ARE

Welcome to the Why Are You So Scared podcast! We are your hosts,
Allison and Tawnie. When we were growing up our dad was in the Navy
so we moved a decent amount. Washington was our dad's final duty
station before retirement. One of the great things about Washington
is that the weather is perfect for building blanket forts and telling
scary stories! Our love for all things creepy and scary grew with us.
So, here we are. Bringing our love for all things creepy to you, our
listeners! Join us as we bring you a new episode every Wednesday as we
share scary stories, their origins or why exactly we are so scared!
