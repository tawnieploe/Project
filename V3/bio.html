WHO WE ARE

Welcome to the Why Are You So Scared podcast! We are your hosts,
Allison and Tawnie. When we were growing up our dad was in the Navy
so we moved a decent amount. Washington was our dad's final duty
station before retirement. One of the great things about Washington
is that the weather is perfect for building blanket forts and telling
scary stories! Our love for all things creepy and scary grew with us.
So, here we are. Bringing our love for all things creepy to you, our
listeners! Join us as we bring you a new episode every Wednesday as we
share scary stories, their origins or why exactly we are so scared!
Growing up I loved hearing stories that would keep me up at night. My love for all things creepy has grown with me and I find that I am constantly looking for the next scary story or the next movie that will keep me up at night. I recently moved back home to Washington with my husband and our 3 Kids upon his retirement from the ARMY. I am a full time student working on my business degree.
From a young age I have always been interested in creepy stories and folklore. Stories that leave you hanging or have a twist in the end, whether they are true or not, no one knows. All I care about is it gives you the chills. I have 3 kids and work as a nurse and a nursing instructor. While I work in a world surrounded by evidence and science, I am also surrounded by a world of miracles and things that have no explanations.
