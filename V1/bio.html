WHO WE ARE

We are Allison and Tawnie, sisters, and hosts of the Why Are You So Scared? podcast! When we were younger our dads carrer in the Navy brought us to his final duty station before retirement in Washington state. One great thing about Washington is the stormy weather setting the perfect atmosphere for blanket forts and scary stories.

As we grew, so did our interest in all things creepy, scary and paranormal. So here we are today bringing our interest to you!
